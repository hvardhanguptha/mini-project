Frequently Asked Questions

What is Liftly?

Liftly is a ride-sharing platform that connects riders and clients within a city.

How do I register?

You can register by providing your details in the registration form.

How does the ride-sharing work?

Once registered, users can select their roles and request rides or offer rides based on availability.

Is there a fee for using Liftly?

Liftly operates on a cost-sharing model; users can split the ride costs.
